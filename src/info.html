How to use website:

Adding teachers / Making personal schedule:

  1. Go to main page and search a teacher you have after you're logged in.
  2. Click on the teacher you have and press the button titled "Add". The button adds the teacher to your schedule list.

Adding friends:

  1. Search friends in main page once logged in
  2. Send friend request, if they requested you will see in your personal schedule page.
  3. Once you accept or they accept, you will be able to see their schedules in your schedule page.

Checking + Uploading Image for Teacher Absence:

Uploading Image

Take a photo on your phone and upload it using the upload button on the side of the teacher's schedule. The image will be processed and soon the teacher's schedule will turn red for his absence.

Is my teacher here?

If your class period is red in your personal schedule, your teacher is not here. OR If you go to their schedule and your class period is in red, your teacher is not here.

IF your teacher is not present but there is a sub, the box will be purple, so go to class please.
